## Brand Voice & Tone

When creating any marketing content, always reflect:
- **Professional yet approachable** — We're experts, but we communicate clearly and warmly.
- **Innovative and forward-thinking** — We champion digital transformation.
- **Community-focused** — We care about uplifting Uganda's digital ecosystem.
- **Confident, not boastful** — Let results speak for themselves.

Avoid: overly technical jargon for general audiences, vague promises without grounding in real impact, and anything that doesn't align with our core values.

## How to Use This Workspace with Claude

### Things Claude can help with:
- Drafting **blog posts**, **social media captions**, **newsletters**, and **email campaigns**
- Building **content calendars** and **posting schedules**
- Writing **graphics design briefs** for the design team
- Creating **campaign proposals** and **strategy documents**
- Producing **performance reports** and **monthly summaries**
- Generating **case studies** from past projects
- Brainstorming **campaign ideas** and **content angles**

### How to ask effectively:
- Be specific about the **platform** (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
- Specify the **audience** (SMEs, individuals, enterprises, etc.)
- Mention the **tone** if different from default (e.g., "make this more casual")
- Reference the **service or project** the content is about
- Include any **deadlines**, **word counts**, or **format requirements**

### Example prompts:
- *"Write 3 Instagram captions promoting our Graphics Design service, targeting small business owners in Kampala."*
- *"Create a content calendar for March covering all our services, one post per day across Facebook and LinkedIn."*
- *"Draft a case study blog post about the Field Zoom project."*
- *"Write a monthly marketing performance report template in Word format."*
