## Logo

| Version | File | Use when |
|---------|------|----------|
| Light (dark ink on white) | `elastic_light_logo_3600px_1028.png` | White/light backgrounds, documents, print |
| White version | Use blue background PDF | Dark/coloured backgrounds, pitch decks |
| White background JPG | `logo-white-background.jpg` | Presentations, email signatures |

### Logo Rules
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo
- Always maintain clear space around the logo (at least the height of the star icon on all sides)
- Minimum digital size: 120px wide
- Do not place on busy backgrounds

## Voice & Tone

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Proposals & client docs | Professional, confident, results-focused | "We deliver scalable software solutions..." |
| Website / marketing | Clear, direct, no jargon | "We build the software your business needs." |
| Social media (TikTok etc.) | Engaging, human, short | "Here's how AI changed how we work..." |
| Internal / with Zoe | Casual, direct | No formality needed |

## Colour Usage in Documents

**Pitch decks & proposals:**
- Cover: Deep Navy (`#1E1E3C`) background, white text and logo
- Body slides: White background, Deep Navy headings, Electric Blue accents
- Accent elements: Electric Blue for callouts, icons, chart highlights

**Email headers:**
- Deep Navy background or white with navy logo

---

## What We Don't Do
- No clipart, no stock-photo-heavy layouts
- No neon colours or gradients (unless Electric Blue → Sky Blue)
- No Comic Sans, Papyrus, or decorative fonts
- No logo on busy/patterned backgrounds
