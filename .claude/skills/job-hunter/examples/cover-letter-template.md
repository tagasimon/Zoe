# Cover Letter Templates

## Structure Rules
- Max 3 paragraphs, under 250 words
- No "I am writing to apply for..." opener — lead with something stronger
- Para 1: Why this role + company (reference something specific from the JD or their work)
- Para 2: 2–3 achievements with numbers that directly match the JD
- Para 3: Short CTA — available, eager, let's talk

## Customisation Notes

When tailoring a cover letter:
1. Replace `[Company Name]` with the actual company name
2. Replace the opening hook with something specific to that company (their industry, a recent project, their JD language)
3. Swap in the 2–3 achievements most relevant to the specific JD
4. Keep Para 3 short — one sentence is enough
