# Outreach Templates

## WhatsApp (preferred — short and direct)

```
Hi [Name], I came across [Company] — impressive work in [industry].

I'm Simon from Elastic Technologies. We help [industry] businesses in Uganda
[specific outcome: e.g. "automate their stock and order management" / "replace spreadsheets with a proper system"].

Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week to see if we can help?
```

---

## Email (slightly longer, more formal)

**Subject:** Helping [Company] with [specific pain point]

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] [specific observation — e.g. "is growing fast in the logistics space" /
"still uses manual processes for fleet tracking"].

We're Elastic Technologies, a Kampala-based software company. We've been helping
[similar companies] [specific outcome — e.g. "cut manual data entry by 80% with a
custom ERP"].

Would a short call this week make sense to explore if we can do the same for you?

Simon Kazooba
Elastic Technologies Ltd
[phone]
```

---

## Rules for Customising

- **Always name a specific thing you observed** — shows you did research
- **Match the outcome to their industry** — logistics pain ≠ healthcare pain
- **One CTA only** — call, not "call or email or visit our website"
- **No attachments on first contact** — only send a proposal after they respond
- **WhatsApp first** in Uganda — email is often ignored
