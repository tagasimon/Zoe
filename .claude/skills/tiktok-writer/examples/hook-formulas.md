# TikTok Hook Formulas

The first 3 seconds determine if someone watches or scrolls. Always write 2-3 hook options and let Simon pick.

---

## Proven Formulas

### Bold Claim
> "This AI tool replaced 4 hours of my work in 10 minutes."
> "I automated my entire client follow-up process for free."

Pattern: `[Specific tool/action] [did something impressive] in [specific time/cost].`

---

### Question Hook
> "Did you know you can do [X] with AI? Most people don't."
> "What if I told you [surprising thing] was possible right now?"

Pattern: `Did you know [surprising fact]? [Contrast with common belief].`

---

### Contrast / Before-After
> "I tried [popular tool] vs [unknown tool]. The result surprised me."
> "6 months ago I was doing this manually. Now it takes 30 seconds."

Pattern: `[Old way] vs [new way]. [Tease the result].`

---

### Story Open
> "Last week I tried something I thought was impossible..."
> "Three days ago a client asked me to do something in 24 hours..."

Pattern: `[Time reference] I [did something risky or unexpected]...`

---

### Challenge / Experiment
> "I gave AI my most boring task. Here's what happened."
> "I challenged myself to run my business using only AI for one week."

Pattern: `I [gave/challenged/asked] AI [to do something specific]. Here's what happened.`

---

### Listicle Tease
> "3 AI tools that changed how I run my business."
> "The 5 things I wish I knew before building my first SaaS."

Pattern: `[Number] [things/tools/mistakes] that [strong outcome].`

---

### Relatable Pain
> "If you've ever spent hours writing proposals, watch this."
> "Tired of chasing clients who never reply? This fixed it for me."

Pattern: `If you've ever [relatable pain], [here's the solution].`

---

## AI Challenge Series — Specific Hooks

For Simon's AI Challenge content, lean into the experiment framing:

- "Day [X] of my AI Challenge. Today I tested..."
- "I tried to [do something] using only AI. Here's the honest result."
- "This AI tool was supposed to [do X]. It actually [did Y]."
- "Nobody talks about [AI tool/use case]. Here's why they should."

---

## What Makes a Hook Fail

- Starting with "Hey guys" or "So today I want to talk about..."
- Starting with context before the payoff
- Being vague ("I found something interesting" — interesting how?)
- Asking a question the viewer doesn't care about yet
