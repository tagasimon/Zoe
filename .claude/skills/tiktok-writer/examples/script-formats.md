# TikTok Script Formats by Length

## 30-Second Script (~75-90 words)

| Section | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Hook | 0-3 sec | Stop the scroll |
| Setup | 3-8 sec | What problem or situation? |
| Value | 8-25 sec | The tip, demo, or reaction |
| CTA | 25-30 sec | Follow, comment, or part 2 tease |

**Template:**
```
[HOOK — 1 sentence]
[VISUAL CUE: ...]

[SETUP — 1-2 sentences]

[VALUE — 3-4 punchy sentences]
[VISUAL CUE: ...]

[CTA — 1 sentence]
```

---

## 60-Second Script (~150-180 words)

| Section | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Hook | 0-3 sec | Stop the scroll |
| Setup | 3-10 sec | Context |
| Value | 10-50 sec | 2-3 punchy points or mini-story |
| CTA | 50-60 sec | Engage or follow |

**Template:**
```
[HOOK — 1 sentence]
[VISUAL CUE: ...]

[SETUP — 2-3 sentences]

[POINT 1 — 2-3 sentences]
[VISUAL CUE: ...]

[POINT 2 — 2-3 sentences]
[VISUAL CUE: ...]

[POINT 3 or payoff — 1-2 sentences]

[CTA — 1 sentence]
```

---

## 90-Second Script (~225-270 words)

Same as 60-sec but the Value section gets a third point or a deeper story beat. Use when the topic genuinely needs more context — don't pad.

---

## Visual Cue Format

Use `[VISUAL CUE: description]` inline to indicate what Simon should show on screen:

- `[VISUAL CUE: face cam, reacting]`
- `[VISUAL CUE: screen record of the tool]`
- `[VISUAL CUE: text overlay: "3 HOURS SAVED"]`
- `[VISUAL CUE: cut to phone showing result]`
- `[VISUAL CUE: point at camera]`

---

## CTA Options (pick one per video)

| Goal | CTA |
|------|-----|
| Build followers | "Follow for part 2." |
| Drive comments | "Drop a comment — have you tried this?" |
| Tease next video | "Part 2 drops tomorrow." |
| Save rate | "Save this before you forget." |
| Shares | "Send this to someone who needs to see it." |
