# Offer Builder

Turn a skill or product into something sellable today.

**Input:** $ARGUMENTS

---

## Context

**Seller:** Simon Kazooba — Elastic Technologies Ltd, Kampala, Uganda
**Products available:**
- Polish My CV — AI CV optimizer (live on Vercel, payments working)
- Website audits — manual + AI-assisted audit with report
- Web/app development — custom websites, mobile apps, automations
**Constraint:** Fast income needed. Not perfection. Sell it now, improve later.

---

## Step 1 — Define the Customer and Problem

- Who is the exact person buying this? (Be specific — not "businesses", say "restaurant owners in Kampala who have no website")
- What is the one pain they feel right now that this solves?
- Why do they need it today, not next month?

---

## Step 2 — Build the Offer

Define each component:

| Component | What to decide |
|-----------|----------------|
| What they get | List deliverables simply — no jargon |
| Price | Uganda price (UGX) + optional global price (USD) |
| Delivery time | How fast can Simon realistically deliver this? |

Price guidance:
- Uganda market: UGX 50,000–500,000 for quick-turn services
- Global market: $20–$150 for productized services
- When unsure, price on the low end and raise after first 3 sales

---

## Step 3 — Add the Hook and Risk Reducer

**Hook:** One sentence that makes someone stop scrolling and want to know more. Lead with the outcome, not the process.

**Risk Reducer:** Remove the reason they hesitate. Options:
- Money-back guarantee (if delivery fails)
- Free preview or sample before payment
- Fast turnaround promise ("done in 24 hours")
- Pay after delivery (for trusted contacts)

---

## Output Format

## Offer Name:
[Short, clear name — what you're selling]

## Target Customer:
[One sentence — who exactly is buying this]

## Problem:
[What pain they have right now]

## Solution:
[What they get — bullet points, simple language]

## Price:
- Uganda: UGX [amount]
- Global: USD [amount] (if applicable)

## Delivery:
[How long it takes from payment to delivery]

## Hook:
[One line that makes them want it]

## Risk Reducer:
[What removes their hesitation]

## One-Line Pitch:
[The full offer in one sentence — customer + problem + solution + outcome]

---

## Rules

- No jargon. If a market trader in Owino can't understand it, rewrite it.
- Must be sellable today — no "coming soon", no "once I build X"
- Price must reflect Uganda market reality unless targeting global explicitly
- Delivery time must be honest — under-promise, over-deliver
- The one-line pitch must stand alone. Read it without context. Does it work? If not, rewrite it.
