# Revenue Audit

Cut to what makes money. Eliminate everything else.

**Input:** $ARGUMENTS

---

## Context

**Who this is for:** Simon Kazooba — Elastic Technologies Ltd, Kampala, Uganda
**Current ventures:**
- SchoolConnect — active client, 1,000,000 UGX outstanding (overdue)
- Polish My CV — live on Vercel, payments working, needs Supabase persistence
- StatusCash — WhatsApp Status ad marketplace, withdrawal flow pending
- Website audit outreach — cold outreach to Kampala businesses
- Rotarise — paused
- Vee Eye Pee — paused

**Known failure mode:** New ideas pull attention before existing ones generate cash. Simon needs to finish before starting.

**Metric that matters:** Speed to cash. If it doesn't bring money in the next 30 days, it is not a priority.

---

## Step 1 — Categorize Every Input

For each activity or product, assign one category:

**Revenue Generating** — actively producing or immediately convertible to cash
- Has a paying customer or imminent payment
- Direct path: do the thing → get paid

**Potential** — could generate revenue but hasn't yet
- Needs one more step (ship feature, send message, close lead)
- Timeline to first cash: under 30 days if focused

**Waste** — consuming time with no realistic path to near-term revenue
- Building features no one has asked for
- Researching instead of selling
- Working on paused ventures
- Anything that makes Simon feel productive but produces no cash

Be ruthless. "Potential" is not a permanent category — if it's been potential for more than 2 weeks, it's waste.

---

## Step 2 — Recommend Focus

**Double down on:** The 1–2 things with the clearest, fastest path to cash. Name them specifically.

**Pause immediately:** Everything else. Not "reduce time on" — pause. A paused thing gets zero hours until the focused thing generates cash.

---

## Step 3 — Assign the Top 2 Actions

The highest ROI actions available right now. Not strategies. Specific actions Simon can complete today or tomorrow.

Format:
1. [Exact action] — expected outcome — by [when]
2. [Exact action] — expected outcome — by [when]

If the SchoolConnect 1M UGX is still outstanding — that is always Action 1.

---

## Output Format

## Revenue Drivers:
[What is actually generating or about to generate cash — be specific]

## Potential:
[What could generate cash within 30 days if Simon acts — with condition]

## Waste:
[What is consuming time with no near-term cash path — name it directly]

## Focus:
[What Simon must double down on — and what to pause immediately]

## Next Actions:
1. [Action] — [outcome] — by [when]
2. [Action] — [outcome] — by [when]

---

## Rules

- Be ruthless. Feelings about projects are irrelevant.
- Speed to cash is the only metric. Not potential, not interest, not growth.
- If something has been "almost ready" for more than 2 weeks — it's waste until proven otherwise.
- No more than 2 things in focus at once. More = none of them ship.
- If Simon's list is mostly waste — say that directly.
