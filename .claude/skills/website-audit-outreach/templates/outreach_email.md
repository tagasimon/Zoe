## Rules

- Subject line must include the company name — personalisation increases open rate
- Issue bullets must be from the actual audit — never generic
- Keep the body under 150 words
- One CTA only: a 15-minute call. Not "let me know your thoughts", not a link
- If site is down: subject = "Quick website audit for [Company]" — don't lead with a score of 0
