# Zoe Life Coach Questions

_Last updated: 2026-05-03_

These are the questions Simon should answer to make Zoe simpler, sharper, and more effective.

## Income And Survival

1. How much money must come in each month for you to stop feeling financially unstable?
2. What is the minimum monthly survival number?
3. What is the comfortable monthly number?
4. Which matters more for the next 30 days: job income, client income, or both equally?
5. What is your current cash on hand?
6. What debts are overdue right now?
7. Who owes you money?
8. Who do you owe money to?
9. What payment conversations are you avoiding?
10. What is the daily money action until income stabilises?

## Business

1. You said the business is running well. What exactly is working?
2. What is not working?
3. What service is easiest to sell right now: websites, mobile apps, automations, ERP, or AI workflows?
4. What is your fastest offer that can close within 7 days?
5. What is your minimum acceptable project fee?
6. Which clients or prospects already trust you?
7. Who can introduce you to paying clients?
8. What follow-up have you delayed because it feels uncomfortable?
9. What would make Elastic Technologies stable for the next 90 days?
10. What work should Elastic stop offering for now?

## Job Search

1. What kind of job would you accept immediately?
2. What salary is the minimum worth accepting?
3. Are you open to onsite Kampala roles, remote only, or both?
4. Are you willing to pause some ventures if a job is secured?
5. What roles fit you best: Flutter, full-stack, AI automation, technical product, ERP, or IT lead?
6. How many applications per week is realistic?
7. Do you want Zoe to prepare applications daily?
8. Who can refer you?
9. What application tasks do you avoid most?
10. What would make you reject a job?

## Procrastination

1. What do you usually do when avoiding work?
2. What type of task triggers the most avoidance?
3. Do you avoid selling, coding, finishing, asking for money, or being judged?
4. What time of day do you produce your best work?
5. What time of day is usually wasted?
6. What apps or habits pull you away?
7. What should Zoe say when she catches you avoiding?
8. Do you respond better to a strict tone, supportive tone, or direct challenge?
9. What is the smallest task that usually gets you moving?
10. What consequence should happen if you miss daily shutdown?

## Project Overload

1. Which projects are truly active this month?
2. Which projects are emotionally hard to pause?
3. Which project has the clearest path to money?
4. Which project has the least clear path to money?
5. Which project are you using as a hiding place?
6. What is the maximum number of active ventures Zoe should allow?
7. What does "paused" mean for you?
8. What does "killed" mean for you?
9. What evidence is enough to continue a venture?
10. What evidence is enough to pause it?

## Life And Habits

1. What daily habits have the biggest effect on your work?
2. What minimum sleep makes you functional?
3. What spending behaviour causes the most regret?
4. What personal obligations must Zoe remember?
5. What relationship/family responsibilities are non-negotiable?
6. What health issue should not be ignored?
7. What does a good day look like outside work?
8. What does a bad day usually start with?
9. What should Zoe track every day?
10. What is too private for Zoe to track?

## Simplicity

1. Which folders do you actually open weekly?
2. Which folders feel like clutter?
3. Which folders make you feel guilty?
4. Which trackers will you realistically update?
5. Do you prefer Markdown, CSV, Google Sheets, or a mix?
6. Should Zoe keep everything local, or sync some trackers to Google Drive?
7. Should old projects be archived aggressively?
8. What should be visible on the dashboard every morning?
9. What should never be on the dashboard?
10. What would make Zoe feel lighter instead of heavier?

## The Three Hard Questions

1. Are you using Zoe to become more productive, or to feel productive?
2. What are you afraid will happen if you focus on only one or two things?
3. If Zoe could force one behaviour for the next 30 days, what should it be?
