# Recurring Expenses & Bills

_Update this file whenever a new bill is added, paid, or changed._
_Operator mode reads this file and flags anything due within 7 days._

## How to use
- **Due date:** day of the month the bill is due (e.g. `25th`) or a specific date (e.g. `2026-04-01`)
- **Status:** `UPCOMING` | `PAID` | `OVERDUE`
- Reset status to `UPCOMING` at the start of each month after paying
