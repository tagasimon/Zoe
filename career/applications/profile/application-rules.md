# Application Rules

## Autonomy

Zoe may search, score, tailor, draft, and prepare application packets. Zoe must not submit applications without Simon confirmation.

## Fit Priorities

- First priority: software, full-stack, mobile, Flutter, Android, iOS, product engineering.
- Secondary priority: AI automation, RPA, workflow automation, data/BI, ERP, systems analysis.
- Company boost: product companies, SaaS, fintech, marketplaces, AI/product teams, engineering-led companies.
- Location: remote worldwide, Uganda, East Africa, and any role that is realistically accessible from Kampala.
- Seniority: mid-senior by default; include junior only when compensation or strategic value is unusually strong.

## Red Flags

- Long unpaid tests before a real screening call.
- Vague company identity or unclear application destination.
- Requests for salary history, references, ID, passport, certificates, or unrelated documents before Simon approves.
- Jobs with no useful job description.
- Roles that are obviously internship-only or too junior with no upside.

## Compensation

Do not reject a role only because salary is hidden. Mark salary as unknown in notes and preserve the opportunity if the role is otherwise strong.

## Tracker Rule

`Applied` means Simon confirmed submission. Drafts, generated packets, and portal instructions are not applications yet.
