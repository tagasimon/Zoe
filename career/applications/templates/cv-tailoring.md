# Tailored CV Template

Use the master profile as source of truth. Do not invent facts.

## Tailoring Rules

- Rewrite the professional summary to mirror the job description.
- Reorder skills so exact JD matches appear first.
- Surface the most relevant real achievements first.
- Keep employer names, job titles, and dates accurate.
- Keep Simon's founder/product work visible, especially for product, startup, automation, and software roles.
- Use direct, ATS-friendly wording.

## Output Shape

```markdown

## Contact

[3-4 sentences tailored to the role]

## Achievements Relevant to Role

[most relevant achievements]

Available upon request.
```
