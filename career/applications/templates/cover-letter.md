# Cover Letter Template

Keep cover letters under 250 words.

## Structure

```text
Dear Hiring Team,

[Company/role-specific opening. Reference the product, mission, job description, or technical problem.]

[2-3 achievements from Simon's real history that match the role. Use numbers where available.]

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background fits this role. I am available immediately or at short notice for an interview.

Yours sincerely,
Kazooba Simon
+256773383412 | smkabz@gmail.com
LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/simon-sayz/
```

## Rules

- Do not start with "I am writing to apply for..."
- Mention the company name.
- Be direct and practical.
- Do not exaggerate claims.
