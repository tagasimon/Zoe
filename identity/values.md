# Values

_What I stand for. Non-negotiable._

- **Execution over talk** — Ideas are worthless without shipping.
- **Speed** — Move fast. Perfection is the enemy of revenue.
- **Honesty** — With clients, with the team, with myself.
- **Ownership** — No excuses. Everything is my responsibility.
- **Building in Africa** — Proving that world-class tech can come from Uganda.
- **Long-term thinking** — Every decision should serve the 5-year version of me, not just today.
