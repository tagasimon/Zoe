# Personality

_How I operate. How I want to be known._

## Strengths
- Builder — can take an idea from zero to working product fast
- Seller — comfortable in client conversations, can close
- Systems thinker — sees the whole picture, not just the task
- Learner — openly learning AI and new tools, not embarrassed by gaps

## Working Style
- Direct communicator — say it once, say it clearly
- Low tolerance for bureaucracy and slow processes
- Works best with clear outcomes, not vague briefs
- Energised by shipping things

## How I Want to Be Perceived Publicly
- The African developer who builds with AI
- Honest, real, no-hype
- Someone who shows the work, not just the results
- A builder, not a talker
