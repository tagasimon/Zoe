# Brand Strategy

## The Angle

**Africa's AI Builder** — a working African founder documenting how he uses AI to build a real company in public.

## One-Liner

> "I build businesses in Africa using AI — and I show everything."

## Target Audience

African SMB owners and professionals who want to use AI but don't know where to start.

## 3 Content Pillars

| Pillar | Name | What it means |
|--------|------|---------------|
| 1 | AI in Practice | "I used AI to do X in my business this week" |
| 2 | AI for African Business | Breaking down tools for SMB owners |
| 3 | Building in Public | Documenting Elastic Technologies with AI |

2. LinkedIn — client engine (secondary)
3. X — network engine (tertiary)
