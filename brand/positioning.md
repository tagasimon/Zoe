# Brand Positioning

## Position Statement

For African SMB owners and professionals who want to use AI but feel overwhelmed —
Simon Kazooba is the practitioner-builder who shows exactly how to use AI in a real African business,
unlike Western AI influencers who teach theory without the African context.

## The Moat

- First-mover: "AI for African business" niche is wide open
- Practitioner credibility: live company being built on camera
- Authentic story: African accent, African problems, African solutions
- Dual engine: brand feeds business, business feeds brand
