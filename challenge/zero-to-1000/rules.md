# Zero to 1000 — Rules

## What This Is

A public challenge: go from 0 to $1,000/month in revenue from digital products or content —
documented in public on TikTok, LinkedIn, and X.

## The Rules

1. Everything is documented publicly — wins, failures, revenue, tools
2. No hiding numbers — post real revenue figures
3. Build in public — show the product, the process, the mistakes
4. One post minimum per week about the challenge
5. Revenue must come from digital products or content (not client services)
6. No paid advertising until month 3

## What Counts as Revenue

- Digital product sales (courses, templates, guides)
- Newsletter sponsorships
- Brand deals / sponsored content
- Affiliate commissions

## What Does Not Count

- Client services (Elastic Technologies revenue)
- Employment income
- Investment

_To be set when challenge officially begins._

## End Condition

Hit $1,000 in a single calendar month from qualifying sources.
